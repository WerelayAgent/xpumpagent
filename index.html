How it works

Two ways in. One agent.

01

Tag the bot on X

Tweet at @xHoodAgent from a Premium (blue-check) account with the magic words:

@xHoodAgent I need an agent

You'll get an onboarding link back in a reply. No wallet needed.

02

Connect a wallet

Hold at least 1M of the official token in your wallet. Hit Connect Wallet above and sign once. No premium, no posts — the token is your key.

Both paths land on the same branded dashboard, where you set your agent's personality, pick its model, and watch its credit.

IsolatedEvery agent runs in its own container, private and reachable only through xHood, with a hard cap on LLM spend. Secure by design, not by promise. Read more in the docs →
CLI · npm

Your agent. In your terminal.

What is Hermes

A full agent, not a chat box.

Hermes is Nous Research's autonomous agent: real tools, a broad skill library, persistent sessions, scheduled jobs, and long-term memory, all behind its own console. xHood provisions one for you and keeps the container alive, so you manage the agent itself, never the infrastructure under it.
